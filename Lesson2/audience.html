Audience

We outside is a website for outdoorsy people, folks who are in love with nature and passionate about connecting with mother nature. The 12year old who wants to know what the weather will be like for a bicycle ride,the 20year old who wants to do goat yoga, and the gentleman who wants to go hiking and do some pushups on the hill. then the grandma who wants to spend qulaity time with hefr grandkids at the park all located within Rexburg area and its environs in the state of Idaho.

Two Personas

Mama Cookie

Mama cookie as her grandkids call her is know for making the best sugar cookies there is, at age 75 she practialy lives for her grandkids. All she wants to do is have the best of time with them at the Rexburg water park, with old age she is concerned about the weather, if its too hot she gets burned and is at great discomfort when sleeping at night. So, We Outside is an app she can use to see the weather forecast and then make informed decsion as to wether she should go outside, the We Outside is easy for her as she has it installed on her Ipad and mobile phone for easy access.

Levi

The one thing Levi likes one than the mountains is seekign his shredded six packs on the mirror. Levi would hike the R mountain top make sure he works hard enough to stay in shape. He has now decided to branch to mountains within the Idho Falls area as well. Like many Levi wants to know if it safe and the weather condition for each day as to be adequately hydrated and enjoy his day. With the We Outside app and its notifications for change in weather conditions, Levi knows he is making an informed decsion as to go outdoors for the day or not. Reason been that the moment the weather changes he will get a notification alerting him of those changes.
