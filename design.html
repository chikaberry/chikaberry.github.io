Contrast

Amazon

    Amazon.com

A good example of color contrast to me as everything within the page highlights the information and beautifies the page. Nothing is lost with in the ranks as each message is viewable and legible.

Proximity

Alibaba

    Alibaba.com

Proximity tends to show relationship, how things are related either by color,category or design.This principle is showcased in this website as things are grouped by category and also design.It makes the viewer to not only wander in the pages but instantly draw the relationship of items on the page.

Alingnment

Typeform

    Typeform.com

A website that actually matches the name of the company, the text and fonts used on this website is so beautifully aligned that it is very pleasing. As you move from top to buttom of the page you can see how the fonts change and the alingments on the page as well. it is a very core principle of design.
